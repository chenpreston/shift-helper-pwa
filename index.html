About SHIFT Helper

Features

SHIFT Helper is a tool to help you manage shifts effortlessly. You can select a DEPOT, plan your weekly shifts, and add them to your calendar. It supports offline use, ensuring you can check your schedule anytime.

Add to Desktop

  • Android: Open the app in Chrome, click the menu (⋮) > "Add to Home Screen".
  • iOS: Open the app in Safari, click the share button (square with arrow) > "Add to Home Screen".
  • Windows / Mac: Open the app in Chrome or Edge, click the menu (⋮ or …) > "Install SHIFT Helper" or "Create Shortcut".

After adding to your desktop, you can use it like a native app and enjoy the convenience of offline shift viewing.

Data and Version

Shift data is based on valid data as of February 22, 2025.
